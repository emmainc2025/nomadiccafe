Our Story

In today’s fast-paced life, we believe people deserve a moment to slow down and truly enjoy the taste of a well-made cup of coffee. Our coffee bike was created to bring that pause — a small break from the rush — where quality and flavor come first. That is how our idea to start this small business was created

For us, coffee is not just a drink. It’s a moment of calm, connection, and enjoyment, served wherever people gather.

Private Events

Mobile coffee catering for private events, parties, and celebrations, serving specialty coffee anywhere.

Weddings

Wedding coffee catering with a stylish coffee bike, offering premium espresso drinks for guests.

Festivals and Markets

Coffee bike service for festivals and markets, delivering fast, high-quality specialty coffee.

Corporate

Corporate coffee catering with a mobile coffee bar for meetings, launches, and company events.

Team Buildings

Coffee catering for team building events, creating relaxed moments with great coffee.

Brand Activities and Pop-Ups

Mobile coffee bike for brand activations and pop-ups, combining specialty coffee with visual impact to attract and engage customers.
